How to make a SCREAMING PUMPKIN for Halloween to scare your unsuspecting neighbors!

A simple DIY project using an Arduino and an Adafruit Music Shield that will have you make a screaming pumpkin for Halloween, and hopefully get a few jump scares out of your unsuspecting neighbors. You can find the Arduino code here: https://alexinflatland.com/how-to-make-a-screaming-pumpkin-for-halloween-to-scare-your-unsuspecting-neighbors/ And here’s a list of the components used and where you can find them: Arduino Uno - https://amzn.to/3BPxbx9 Adafruit Music Maker Shield - https://amzn.to/3vrKcdS Ultrasonic Sensor - https://amzn.to/3aKkMOZ Sound Sensor - https://amzn.to/3j5lpHq Power Bank with a Speaker - https://amzn.to/2Z2vUV1 LEDs and resistors - https://amzn.to/3BSU82u Breadboards - https://amzn.to/3FRL00d
